Emergency Appeal

On the night of Monday 10th April, a fire ripped through the La Liniere Refugee camp, burning down the children's centre, other community spaces and many, many shelters.

We have lost everything in the centre and we desperately need funds to replace resources and buy emergency supplies. The volunteers are hugely important to the children and their continued contact with them at this time cannot be undervalued.

Any donation you can make to help us support the children during this very difficult time is hugely appreciated.
