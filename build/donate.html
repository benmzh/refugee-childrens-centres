Donate Now

Refugee Children’s Centres operate entirely on donations from the public; what we do wouldn’t be possible without the continuing support and generosity of our followers. The money we raise mainly goes towards supporting small teams of long-term volunteers, ensuring that the Centres provide stability and continuity for the children in uncertain and ever-changing environments. Any efforts you make in raising awareness and promoting our cause are vital to keep us going.

Donations of materials and supplies

We welcome donations but to save us being overwhelmed by your generosity – we have very little storage space – please ask before you bring things. We often have very specific needs and it would be wonderful if you could concentrate your efforts on those things.

Our Supporters

Thanks to our sponsors and donors. We are funded entirely by donations – our sponsors and donors are vital for the running of Refugee Children’s Centres. If your charity, organisation or company is interested in sponsoring our work please contact us.
